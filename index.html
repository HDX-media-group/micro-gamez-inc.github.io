Rebranding

Is it a bird? Is it a plane? Is it a flying root beer? NOPE!! It's the Micro rebranding! We are thrilled to announce that our company, formerly known as "MicroDeciml," has transformed into the sleek and innovative brand, Micro! At Micro, we are passionate about creating extraordinary digital experiences. As a leading provider of electronic arts, including games, apps, engines, animation, and more, we strive to push the boundaries of creativity and technology. Our dedicated team of talented artists, developers, and designers work tirelessly to craft immersive and captivating digital content that resonates with audiences worldwide. Why choose Micro? We believe in the power of imagination and its ability to shape the future. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every project we undertake. Whether it's developing cutting-edge games that transport players to new worlds, building intuitive and user-friendly apps that enhance productivity, or crafting powerful game engines that empower developers, our goal is to ignite joy, inspiration, and wonder through our creations. With our rebranding, we aim to encapsulate the essence of our company in a simple, yet impactful name: Micro. It represents our agility, adaptability, and focus on precision. Just like the building blocks of technology, we strive to make a big impact with the smallest details. Explore the new Micro website and immerse yourself in a world of endless possibilities. From the moment you step into our virtual realm, you'll discover a treasure trove of digital marvels awaiting your exploration. Engage with captivating games that ignite your imagination, download innovative apps that simplify your life, access robust game engines to bring your vision to life, and marvel at stunning animations that breathe life into your ideas. We invite you to join us on this exciting journey as we continue to redefine the landscape of electronic arts. Embrace the Micro revolution and unlock the extraordinary. The sky is no longer the limit; it's just the beginning. Welcome to Micro, where boundless digital experiences come to life!
